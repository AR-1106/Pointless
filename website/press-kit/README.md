# Pointless — Press Kit

## One-liner
Cursor without the click. Pointless turns your webcam into a touchless trackpad.

## Elevator (50 words)
Pointless is a macOS menu-bar app that lets you control your cursor with hand gestures. Pinch and release to tap; stay pinched and move your middle finger to scroll. Every frame is analyzed on-device with Apple's Vision framework — nothing is uploaded. It runs alongside your trackpad, not instead of it.

## Description (120 words)
Pointless is a privacy-first hand-gesture cursor for macOS 26 Tahoe. It sits quietly in your menu bar and watches your hand through the built-in camera. Pinch and release for a tap; stay pinched and move your middle finger to scroll. All hand-tracking happens locally using Apple's Vision framework; frames never leave your Mac. Because Pointless uses `CGWarpMouseCursorPosition` only when your hand is actively moving, your trackpad continues to work as normal. Pointless is designed to feel native — Liquid Glass panels, spring animations, system haptics — and ships as a notarized direct download.

## Key facts
- **Platform:** macOS 26 Tahoe or newer, Apple silicon
- **Runtime size:** ~8 MB
- **Permissions:** Camera, Accessibility
- **Price:** Free (donations welcome)
- **Distribution:** Notarized DMG from https://pointless.app
- **Auto-update:** Sparkle 2 (EdDSA-signed appcast)
- **Telemetry:** None by default. Opt-in MetricKit crash reports.

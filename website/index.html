FAQ

Does it work offline?

Yes. Pointless is 100% on-device. It never needs an internet connection to track your hand.

What hardware do I need?

Apple silicon Mac running macOS 26 Tahoe or newer, with a built-in or USB camera.

How much CPU does it use?

About 6-10% on an M-series chip while tracking, idle when tracking is off.

Is it open source?

Not yet. We're focused on shipping polish. If you're interested in contributing, reach out at support@pointless.app.
